# 2026 MN Fair Labor Standards Act (MFLSA) Requirements

## ⚠️ Effective February 2026
**Penalties**: Up to $10,000 per violation

## Mandatory Tracking
1. **15-minute breaks**: Every 4 hours of work
2. **30-minute meal periods**: For shifts 6+ hours
3. **Paid leave accrual**: 0.88% of wages tracking
4. **Record retention**: 3 years minimum

## Project Relevance
This system helps businesses comply with #1-3 while protecting worker privacy.
